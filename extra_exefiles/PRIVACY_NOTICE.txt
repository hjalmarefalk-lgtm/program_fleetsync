FleetSync Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-03-01

FleetSync processes files locally on your device.
We do not collect or transmit your workbook data by default.

What data FleetSync uses:
- Input workbooks and profile files you select.
- Settings stored locally on your device (for example, selected folders and preferences).

What FleetSync does not do by default:
- No telemetry or analytics.
- No automatic upload of your data to external servers.

Output and storage:
- Output files are saved locally, with Downloads used as default output unless you change it.
- Settings are stored locally in your user profile/app data location.

Support and data minimization:
- If you contact support, share only what is needed to diagnose the issue.
- Do not include sensitive personal or customer data unless explicitly requested.

Retention:
- FleetSync does not set a remote retention period because data stays on your device.
- You control retention by deleting or archiving local files and outputs.
